About Us

Our Story

Student owned and operated quick-service breakfast stand on the campus of the University of South Carolina.

Serving fast, easy, and delicious breakfast food to university students and faculty alike. Partnered with Aramark's Carolina Food Company.

Core Values

  1. Quality: We are committed to providing high-quality, delicious breakfast options made from fresh ingredients to fuel our campus community.
  2. Community: We strive to create a welcoming and inclusive environment where everyone feels valued and connected.
  3. Sustainability: We prioritize sustainable practices in our sourcing, preparation, and packaging to minimize our environmental impact.
  4. Customer Service: We are dedicated to delivering exceptional customer service, ensuring every interaction is positive and memorable.
